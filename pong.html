Shefali's Projects Pong (2017)

Our Product

Pong

Pong is a single-player version of the classic Ping Pong game. In this game, a player uses the left and right arrow keys to control the horizontal movement of a paddle, with the goal of bouncing a ball as many times as possible without missing.

As the game progresses, its difficulty increases due to the gradually increasing speed of the ball. The color of the ball and text on the screen, as well as the initial speed of the ball, are set randomly each time the game is started.
