AudioSense - An Exploration of Immersive 3D Sound Scenes

Developed during the 2016 UC Irvine COSMOS research program, AudioSense is an iOS application which uses sound spatialization at various frequencies to test an individual’s ability to localize 3D sounds. This app is coded in Swift 2.0, Apple’s programming language, and employs the use of algorithms, digital sound processing, and 3D content creation.

In addition to the development of this app during the program, my team and I conducted several experiments and authored a research paper on sound localization and sound spatialization. These project components confirm that AudioSense is an accurate method of testing a person’s ability to localize sounds.
